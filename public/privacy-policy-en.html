2. What Data Do We Collect?

2.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

BoatChecker does not collect:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • GPS location or position
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos (stored only locally on your device)
  • Device ID or advertising ID

3. How Do We Use the Data?

Anonymized data is used exclusively for:

  • Community statistics: Showing "common findings" for different boat types
  • Product improvement: Understanding which checkpoints are most relevant
  • Aggregated analysis: General insights about the boat market (no individual data)

We never sell data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your inspections, notes, and photos are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share a report.

4.2 Server Storage

Anonymized community data is stored on servers in the EEA. All communication occurs via encrypted HTTPS connection.

4.3 Retention Period

  • Local data: Until you delete the app or data
  • Anonymized statistics: Retained indefinitely (cannot be linked to you)

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request information about data we have about you (anonymized data cannot be identified)
  • Right to erasure: Delete local data via the app, or contact us
  • Right to withdraw consent: Turn off "Contribute to the community" at any time
  • Right to data portability: Export inspections as PDF
  • Right to complain: To the Norwegian Data Protection Authority (datatilsynet.no)
